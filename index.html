Bio

  • Vítor Lourenço is currently a Master Candidate at Computing Institute, Federal Fluminense University and a Research Intern at IBM Research Brazil. His research interests include machine learning, neural-symbolic integration, and knowledge representation. Particularly, he works with learned representation of graphs, and their applications in knowledge bases completion and reasosing over knowledge bases.
  • Vítor received his B.Sc degree from the Federal Fluminense University.
